Bolognese

Description

Another easy recipe from Italian kitchen, one of my favourites the Bolognese that you can eat with pasta of your choice.
A few ingredients go a long way if you combine them like Italians do.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Oregano

Preparation

  1. Take a cooking pot and put a bit of oil into it
  2. Cut the onion into small cubes and let it sizzle on heat
  3. When they start to look glassy, put the meat in together
  4. Let the meat cook until white
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce
  6. Let it boil and turn the heat down
  7. Sprinkle a bit of salt to your liking
  8. Spice with oregano
