Tomato Basil Soup

Description

This is a simple Tomato Basil soup wich creates that fresh taste in your mouth and makes for a healthy meal.
A perfect appetizer, or combined with crunchy bread and spread, it can be a full meal

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Take a cooking pot and slice the garlic to small pieces and let it sizzle
  2. Put in the tomato sauce and let it boil
  3. Fill in 1l of water
  4. Put in Basil
  5. Sprinkle with salt
  6. Serve
